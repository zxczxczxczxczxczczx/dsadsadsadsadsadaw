Hedging Strategies

Options Contracts

Use put options to protect against price drops by selling at a set price. Ideal for holding Ethereum during volatility.[web:3]

Short Selling

Borrow and sell crypto high, buy back low to offset losses in long positions like Solana.[web:3]

Futures & Perpetuals

Lock in prices without expiration dates for ongoing hedges.[web:3]

Diversification

Mix BTC, ETH, altcoins, stablecoins, and RWAs to spread risk.[web:8]
